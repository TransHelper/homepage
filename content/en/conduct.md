# Code of Conduct

Our commitment to an open, welcoming, and fair community.

---

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making our community a friendly, safe, and fair place for everyone.

We are committed to fostering an environment that respects and promotes the dignity, rights, and contributions of every individual, regardless of race, ethnicity, caste, color, age, physical appearance, neurodiversity, disability, sex, gender identity or expression, sexual orientation, language, philosophical or religious belief, nationality or social origin, socioeconomic status, education, or any other identity attribute.

All individuals who sincerely participate in and abide by this covenant are entitled to equal rights of participation.

## Expected Behavior

- Treat others with empathy and kindness
- Respect differing viewpoints, experiences, and backgrounds
- Give and accept constructive feedback gracefully
- Acknowledge mistakes and learn from them
- Focus on what is best for the community as a whole

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Any form of discrimination, harassment, or bullying
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Sexualized content or unwelcome advances
- Sustained disruption of community discussions or events
- Other conduct which could reasonably be deemed inappropriate

## Enforcement

1. Violators will receive a private warning explaining the violation and what corrective action is expected.
2. If the behavior continues or is severe, the person may be temporarily or permanently expelled from the community.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all community spaces, including but not limited to GitHub repositories, Discord servers, social media channels, and in-person or virtual events. Anyone who violates this Code of Conduct may be sanctioned or expelled at the discretion of the community maintainers.
